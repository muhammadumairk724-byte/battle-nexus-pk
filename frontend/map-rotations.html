Map Details & Strategy

Each map has its own personality. Here's what you need to know:

  • Bermuda: Best for close-to-medium range combat. Use SMGs and shotguns in the dense forest areas.
  • Purgatory: Long sightlines favor snipers and DMRs. Control the high ground for a tactical advantage.
  • Kalahari: Wide open spaces require good rotation and vehicle usage. ARs are the weapon of choice.
  • Alpine: Complex verticality. Snipers dominate the peaks, while SMGs excel in the tight village corridors.
🎯 Stay Updated: Map rotations are subject to change based on tournament format and player feedback. Follow our official channels for real-time updates.
